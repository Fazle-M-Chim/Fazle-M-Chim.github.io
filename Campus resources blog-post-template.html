Essential Campus Resources

November 7, 2024

As a student at Penn State, there are numerous resources available to help you succeed academically, maintain your well-being, and make the most of your college experience. Here are five essential campus resources that every Penn State student should know about.

1. Counseling and Psychological Services (CAPS)

CAPS provides confidential mental health services to Penn State students, including individual counseling, group therapy, crisis intervention, and psychiatric services.

  • Location: Student Health Center, 5th Floor
  • Phone: (814) 863-0395
  • Website: caps.psu.edu
  • Hours: Monday-Friday, 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM
  • 24/7 Crisis Line: 1-877-229-6400

2. Learning Resource Center (LRC)

The LRC offers free tutoring services, study skills workshops, and academic coaching to help students excel in their coursework.

  • Location: 220 Boucke Building
  • Phone: (814) 865-2582
  • Email: LRCinfo@psu.edu
  • Website: pennstatelearning.psu.edu
  • Hours: Monday-Thursday, 9:00 AM - 9:00 PM; Friday, 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM

3. Career Services

Career Services helps students with career planning, resume writing, interview preparation, and job/internship searches.

  • Location: Bank of America Career Services Center
  • Phone: (814) 865-2377
  • Email: CareerServices@psu.edu
  • Website: studentaffairs.psu.edu/career
  • Hours: Monday-Friday, 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM

4. Student Care & Advocacy

This office assists students experiencing personal, academic, or financial difficulties by connecting them with appropriate resources and support services.

  • Location: 120 Boucke Building
  • Phone: (814) 863-2020
  • Email: StudentCare@psu.edu
  • Website: studentaffairs.psu.edu/studentcare
  • Hours: Monday-Friday, 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM

5. Campus Recreation

Penn State Campus Recreation offers fitness facilities, intramural sports, wellness programs, and outdoor adventure activities.

  • Location: 201 Student Recreation Building
  • Phone: (814) 865-4151
  • Email: PSUCampusRec@psu.edu
  • Website: campusrec.psu.edu
  • Hours: Vary by facility; check website for current hours

Remember that these resources are included in your student fees and are available to all Penn State students. Don't hesitate to reach out and take advantage of these valuable services during your time at Penn State.
